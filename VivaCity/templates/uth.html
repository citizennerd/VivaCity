Every day we ha ve more and more the feeling of distance with the governance
With a few friends we started analyzing open data and Linked Open Data
The first approach has a very liquid structure if it has a structure at all, while the second approache has a very structured approach (too structured, some times)
But what was the real issue were the instruments. Socrata in the USA has created the city portal for the open data, but every information is given separately. Not even the LOD approach connects the info. and the instuments are UGLY!!!
What was a thing that enabled users to feel emathy with the city itself? It was a Game: SimCity
As the Sim City developer blog states, the first versions of SC were basically only enormous data-visualization tools with simulated data generated all the time.
So here comes VivaCity: A sim-city-esque interface to a complex data-model that fetches more or less real-time data from the remote data-providers and enables the user to "live" the data.
Oh, and because I want the project to live and to be used and evolved, you are welcome to Fork it and add new data-providers or new visualization methods or anything!!!
